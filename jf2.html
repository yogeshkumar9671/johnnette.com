Johnnette JF2 is a long range stealth drone that gives you 4K live video giving you the real time situational analysis capability you need. JF2 is designed to be rugged and can be used in any terrain. It has a endurance of 60 min and a transmission range of 10 km. JF2 is the fixed-wing drone for all your mapping and surveillance needs.
